Our Mission

Community Ascension Through
Service & Scholarship

Achieve M.O.O.R.E exists to uplift the AUC through philanthropy, comradery, and altruism. We bridge the gap between students and faculty to improve academic performance at Morehouse, while strengthening the surrounding community through mentorship, youth development, and initiatives that inspire entrepreneurship and unity.

Our Values
